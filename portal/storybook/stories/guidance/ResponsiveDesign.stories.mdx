## Responsive design principles

In alignment with the [USWDS' "Promote continuity" design principle](https://designsystem.digital.gov/design-principles/#promote-continuity), we always strive to provide critical functionality and minimize disruption regardless of the devices used. To accomplish this goal, for new designs, we ask ourselves the following questions:

1. Is the design responsive to effectively and efficiently be accessed on various device sizes?
1. Is the experience consistent on any browser and device? If not, is there strong rationale to support that decision?
